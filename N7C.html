N7Consulting: Junior company at ENSEEIHT

Our goal is to put the students of the school in contact with professionals for the realization of technical missions in the fields of data processing, hydraulics and electronics. My role is to prospect for clients and to sign the missions. Then I take care of the follow-up of the project by realizing meetings with the customers, by showing the deliverables and by writing administrative documents. In addition, we carry out internal projects to develop our sales and marketing strategy. Thanks to our work, we are in the top 3 of the best junior companies in France alongside Centrale Supelec and TBS. I was in charge of an application for a hospital in Toulouse to help cancer patients take their medication.I also worked with two students from the school for a space research center. They worked on a database. This post allowed me to discover the business world, prospecting, customer relations, and taking initiative. The goal is to acquire soft skills in addition to my hard skills.
