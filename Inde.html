First year intership as a volunteer in India

My internship in India as a humanitarian volunteer was a rich and exciting experience. I had the opportunity to work with an international development organization that aims to improve the living conditions of disadvantaged populations in the country. My internship took place in a village in southern India, in the region of Tamil Nadu. I worked closely with community members and volunteers from the organization to implement projects that aimed to improve access to education, clean water, and healthcare. I also participated in awareness activities on human rights and the fight against discrimination. This internship allowed me to discover a very different culture from my own and to face new challenges. I was deeply touched by the kindness and warm welcome of the local population, who made me feel like a full member of the community. In writing this report, I hope to share with you my experience and reflections on this humanitarian internship in India. I also hope that it will inspire you to engage in this type of project and contribute to the fight against inequalities and injustices in the world
